target-owner-vocab
==================

**Consumer** (C)

    An individual that purchases and makes use of a product or service

**Employee** (E)

    An individual internal to an organization who facilitates the disbursement of products or services

**Organization** (O)

    The organization, rather than its employees or customers, owns the target

**Third Party** (T)

    Parties external to an organization and consumers

**Other** (Z)

    Other owner not listed in this vocabulary
