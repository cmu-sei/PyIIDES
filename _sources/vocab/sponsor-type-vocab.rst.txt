sponsor-type-vocab
==================

**Organized Crime** (OC)

    E.g., terrorist group, mafia, gang

**State Sponsor** (SS)

    Geopolitical, state, or national military with a speciific country of origin

**Foreign National** (FN)

    An individual whose objectives are aligned with either the political, commercial, or military interests of a country other than where the incident originated

**Criminal Enterprise** (CE)

    A group of individuals with an identified hierarchy or structure who engage in significant criminal activity

**Other** (OT)

    A sponsor type not listed in this vocabulary
