recruitment-vocab
=================

**Avoid Detection** (1)

    Insider1 needed insider2's access or authorization to avoid detection

**Greater Payoff** (2)

    Insider1 received a greater payoff by recruiting insider2

**Insider Resigned** (3)

    Insider1 no longer worked at the victim organization and needed insider2 to finish the incident

**Separation of Duties** (4)

    Insider1 could not conduct acheive their goals without insider2 due to a separation of duties which limited their access of authorization

**Share the Wealth** (5)

    Insider1 recruited insider2 due to a desire to share the wealth

**Other** (9)

    Insider1 recruited insider2 for unknown reasons
