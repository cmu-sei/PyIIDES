channel-vocab
=============

**Company Email** (1)

    An email account the company controls

**Company Phone** (2)

    A phone the company controls

**Instant Messaging** (3)

    Direct, synchronous or asynchronous communication via app, software, or web

**Discussion Forum** (4)

    Private or public forum accessed via the Internet for the purposes of discussing a particular topic or theme

**Personal Email** (5)

    An email account the organization does not control or monitor

**Personal Phone** (6)

    A phone the organization does not control or monitor

**Social Media** (7)

    Websites and applications that enable users to create and share content or to participate in social networking

**Other** (9)

    Other type of channel not listed in this vocabulary
