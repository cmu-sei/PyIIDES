technical-control-vocab
=======================

**Access to Technical System(s) Restricted** (1)

    Insider's access to the organization's technical systems is restricted in any capacity (e.g., the organization declares that insider could only log onto system if another employee is present)

**Access to Technical System(s) Terminated** (2)

    Insider's access to the organization's technical systems is completely terminated (e.g., the insider's account is deleted upon termination from the company)
