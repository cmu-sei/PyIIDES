attack-location-vocab
=====================

**On-site** (1)

    Action taken while on site at an organizational facility

**Remotely** (2)

    Action taken while remote (i.e., not at an organizational facility)

**Remotely from home** (3)

    Action taken remotely while at the insider's or accomplice's home

**Remotely while traveling** (4)

    Action remotely while traveling
