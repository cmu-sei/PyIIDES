marital-status-vocab
====================

**Single** (1)

    Not currently married

**Married** (2)

    Currently belonging to a marriage or common law partnership with a spouse who is an intimate partner

**Divorced** (3)

    Having legally exited a previous marriage

**Separated** (4)

    Married but no longer in an intimate partnership with a spouse

**Widowed/Widower** (5)

    Previously in a marriage that ended due to the death of their spouse
